# Sard (سرد) · Brand Identity

**Name.** Sard, Arabic *سرد*: narration, the flow of a story. The product turns the
spoken chaos of a meeting into a clean, trustworthy narrative. The Arabic wordmark
appears beside the Latin one as a first-class element, never as decoration.

**One-liner.** Your meetings, narrated.

**Positioning.** The private, honest alternative to cloud meeting bots: transcription
in the browser, AI on your hardware, memory only when you approve it, and every claim
cited. Notion-level polish, Otter-level convenience, zero-trust-level privacy.

## Voice

- Calm, literate, precise. Sard writes like a careful editor, not a hype deck.
- Honesty is the feature: "not in the transcript" is said proudly.
- No em dashes. Use commas, colons, or a middle dot (·).
- Bilingual accents (English first, Arabic as identity), RTL-ready by design.

## Color

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Ink | `#221d16` | Text, dark blocks, logo field |
| Paper (canvas) | `#faf6ef` | Background |
| Surface | `#f2ecdf` / soft `#f7f2e8` | Panels, cards |
| Hairline | `#e5dcc9` / strong `#cbbfa6` | Borders |
| Saffron (primary) | `#b3541e` · pressed `#94431a` | Actions, citations, the نقطة dot |
| Slate / Steel / Stone | `#57503f` / `#7d7460` / `#a89d87` | Secondary text |
| Tints | sand `#ece4d2` · sky `#dfe8e4` · olive `#e4e8d4` · peach `#f3e0d0` | Capture cards, chips |

Feel: warm paper and dark ink, a manuscript rather than a dashboard. Saffron is the
single accent; it marks actions and *citations*, the brand's proof-of-honesty.

## Typography

- **Display:** Fraunces (variable, optical sizing). Headlines, brand, prices.
- **UI/body:** Inter.
- **Arabic:** Noto Naskh Arabic for سرد and Arabic passages.
- Timestamps are part of the brand (they are citations); keep them visible.

## Logo

`public/favicon.svg`: a rounded ink square, a paper-colored stroke of three teeth
(the س) flowing into a wide bowl, and one saffron dot. It reads as sound-wave,
signature, and Arabic letter at once. Clear space: half the mark's width. Never
recolor the dot.

## Motion

Ease `cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)`. Views fade-up 400ms; cards lift 2 to 4px on
hover; landing sections reveal on scroll. Always honor `prefers-reduced-motion`.
