Meeting notes that narrate themselves.

Sard listens so you can stay present. It transcribes in your browser, writes structured notes with your own local AI, and links every summary, decision, and task to the exact moment it was said. Nothing ever leaves your machine.

Get Sard on GitHub, free Run it in 2 commands

100% local. No account, no cloud, no telemetry. Open source.

# runs entirely on your machine
git clone https://github.com/AbdulrahmanAlaasi/sard && cd sard
npm install && npm run dev
# open http://localhost:5173 and start a meeting
Private by architecture: Whisper transcribes on-device, and notes are written by your own model via Ollama, LM Studio, Jan, or llamafile.

Everything a notetaker should do, without the cloud.

Capture the meeting however it happens, then let Sard write its story.

🎙️

Record the room or the tab

Microphone for in-person meetings. Tab capture for Zoom and Meet in the browser, mixed with your mic. Upload audio or paste a transcript. No bots join your calls.

✍️

On-device transcription

Whisper runs inside your browser with an automatic precision fallback, live progress, and a choice of model sizes. The audio never leaves your computer.

🧾

Notes with receipts

Summary, key points, decisions, and action items in a clean, Notion-style layout. Owners and due dates are captured when they were said, never invented.

🗂️

A tidy meeting library

Meetings grouped by day, full-text search across titles, summaries, and transcripts, editable titles, and one-click Markdown export.

How it works.

Capture

Record mic or tab audio, upload a file, or paste text.

Transcribe

Whisper runs in the browser via WebGPU or WASM, with timestamps.

Narrate

Your local model (auto-detected: Ollama, LM Studio, Jan, llamafile) writes the structured notes.

Keep

Everything is stored in your browser's local database. Export any meeting as Markdown.
